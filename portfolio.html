Stockbuddy

Github Repo Link

My first large project, this app is used to help beginners enter the stock market.
Once the user has typed a stock symbol of their choice into the search engine it will give a pinnable card as the search result. They can then pin this card to their "workspace", and use the pinned stock to view trends, news, and other important information relating to that company.
