Welcome to my personal portfolio!

My name is Louis Yacksmith and I am a professional Web Designer living in Ottawa, Ontario. I am a jack of all trades, and through a variety of jobs ranging from a fine dining chef all the way to retail, I am confident with my ability to create a positive and highly motivated work environment. Craving more in life and a career that I can be happy with I set out to earn my degree in Web Development at Carleton University, and that was the start of a lifelong passion for coding and the creativity that comes hand in hand with developing websites.
This website is an quick example of some of the skills I have gained through my experiences.
